Privacy Policy — Polaris "Attach Prof Inv to Sales Order" browser extension

Last updated: 20 July 2026 · Published by JLS Yachts LLC / New Horizon IT

This extension is an internal business tool for JLS Yachts staff. It adds a button to QuickBooks Online Sales Order pages that attaches a company-generated Pro-Forma PDF fetched from the company's own Polaris platform.

What the extension accesses

  • On QuickBooks Online pages (qbo.intuit.com) it reads the customer name shown on the Sales Order form, solely to find the matching Pro-Forma document.
  • It communicates only with the company's own Polaris server (jls-navigator.m-peeters-4a0.workers.dev) to fetch Pro-Forma PDFs.

What the extension stores and transmits

  • Your extension settings (the Polaris URL, an access token, and the name you enter) are stored in your browser's extension storage.
  • Basic usage telemetry is sent to the company's Polaris server: the name you entered, the extension version, and whether an attach succeeded or failed (including the error message shown to you). This is used by company IT to support the tool.

What the extension does NOT do

  • It never reads, stores, or transmits your QuickBooks credentials — file uploads are performed by the QuickBooks page itself inside your existing signed-in session.
  • It does not collect browsing history, does not run on any site other than QuickBooks Online, and sends no data to any third party.
  • No data is sold or shared outside the company.
